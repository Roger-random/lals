    Sunday 2025/12/07

    December 7, 2025

    Notes#

    Whenever I’m ready to upgrade from my starter whistle, Ronne recommends Acme Thunderer 58 or Titanic.

    Liz fired up her Allen Models of Nevada 2-6-0 Mogul coal-burning(!) steam locomotive but unfortunately some misplaced rods meant she couldn’t run it. Liz was very friendly and answered questions as I watched her go through procedure to shut her engine back down for the night.

    Liz loves the K-27 locomotive, specificially engine 463, and was very excited to hear I’m contemplating building Kozo Hiraoka’s K-27. Sounds like I’ll have an appreciative fan if I go make one a 463.

    Since we were on the topic of favorite locomotives, I expressed my eventual goal is to have a 4-8-4 Northern. Preferably the Santa Fe 3751 but I would not complain about Santa Fe 2926. In response Nick G. answered his favorite Northern was the Southern Pacific 4360.
